Welcome jj

June 2022

It has been a year and a half since we announced that Kogswell is returning. That return has been moving slowly. But conditions are good and we seem to be picking up speed.

For those who are new to Kogswell, our goal is to make it easy for people to use their bikes for transportation. We do that by building a community of daily cyclists and then using their extraordinary talents to do things like designing bicycle widgets using open-source methods. So if you ride a bike and/or have bicycle design chops then consider yourself one of us.

We hope that veteran Kogswell kampaigners find a place here as well. There would not have been a Kogswell without their contributions and I would be happy to hear what they have been doing.

So thanks for visiting and please come back soon.

-M(atthew)
